Roll The Dice

Rules

The rule of the game is for you to reach 10 Wins before the computer does.

Every 20 Score points is 1 Win point.

Each roll score is accumilated in the Score section until you get 20.

Apart from 6 regular dice sides, you'll have two jokers:
- Colored (x2) doubles your current score;
- B&W (x0) turns your current score to zero;

Before you can play the game, make sure you hit the "Roll" button below dices in middle section.
Good Luck!
